Verilog & Netlist Training Datasets to Accelerate AI for Chip Design

We provide over 100B tokens of raw Verilog and SystemVerilog novel code that SOTA models have not been trained on. The training data includes RTL and testbenches (both synthesizable and behavioral) across GPUs, TPUs, ASICs, FPGAs, NVIDIA DLAs, AMD Instinct architectures, and related systems.

It is a full-spectrum corpus for ML/RL training in semiconductors, focused on accelerating chip design for AI and improving HDL / UVM code generation. The dataset is specifically curated to help models master difficult chip design challenges, including floorplanning, square packing, timing closure, clock domain crossing (CDC), and power-aware RTL optimization.

Access can be structured as non-exclusive training, or exclusive (field-limited or time-bound) licenses, depending on your needs. All options restrict redistribution of raw data, while ensuring your team retains full ownership of any trained models and outputs.
