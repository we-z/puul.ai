  • Arm Cortex / Neoverse & RISC-V cores: Out-of-order pipelines, privilege modes, ISA extensions, and SoC integration logic for mobile through server-class chips

Interconnects, memory & IO:

  • CXL & cache-coherent fabrics: Coherent memory pooling, switch logic, and protocol engines for disaggregated datacenter architectures

  • Power-aware RTL & DVFS: Multi-voltage island design, power gating sequences, and dynamic voltage-frequency scaling controllers
